About Us

Shaino Italia: The Brand that Serves.

Shaino Italia® was established in 1999, and mainly concentrated in texture painting, Interior Designing, Building Pilling, wall cladding etc. Over the years, through a motto of non-compromise on quality, we have now acquired a credible and countable position of a leading company, Made in Italy. We are the 1st texture founder in this industry, that is our valuable credit. We have texture work institution, no age limitation. We are all focused on, and commited to customer satisfaction.

Cost-effective solutions, and timely product delivery, are two of our main assets. The flexibility of our production process, based on cold forming, enables us to offer our customers a product carefully tailored on their requirements. We understand our clients and always try to deliver the expectations and more at its best.

Creation, redevelopment, reconstruction, protection or re-equipment of residential premises - Shaino Luxury offers suitable products for any project or any planes; ceilings, walls, floors and much more.

Our Vision

Shaino Italia produces high-quality paints with an emphasis on innovation and eco-friendliness. We are driven by the philosophy to consistently deliver paint solutions that serve your needs.

Why choose us?

Textures are not second options anymore. They add more dynamics to a room and make it stand out.
